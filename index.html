The Shardbearers of Elden Ring

Image Description Stats
Godrick, The Grafted

The first shardbearer you encounter, Godrick is a misbegotten demigod, and the ruler of Stormveil Castle. He gets the title "The Grafted" due to the fallen warriors which he has grafted onto himself, explaining the high amounts of limbs over his body.
  • Health- 6,080 HP
  • Defense- 105
  • Special Attack- Stormcaller
Rennala, Queen of The Full Moon

The second shardbearer you encounter, Rennala is a sorceress, and the leader of the Academy of Raya Lucaria. Her title "Queen of The Full Moon" comes from her status as the Lunar Queen and as a powerful sorceress capable of moon-related powers, due to her bloodline, the Carians, being a family of very powerful sorcerers.
  • Health- 3,493 HP
  • Defense- 109
  • Special Attack- Rennala's Full Moon
Starscourge Radahn

The third shardbearer you encounter, Radahn is a demigod, although weakened due to something known as Scarlet Rot. He gains the name "Starscourge" due to his space-related spells, which mostly involve gravity manipulation.

  • Health- 9,572 HP
  • Defense- 113
  • Special Attack- Meteor Crash
Rykard, Lord of Blashphemy

The fourth shardbearer you encounter, Rykard is demigod, and the lord of the Volcano Manor. He is regarded as the "Lord of Blashphemy" due to his standing, which rejects the usual faith in the Golden Order, which simply functions as a major government and religion.
  • Health- 59,174 HP
  • Defense- 115
  • Special Attack- Magma Thrash
Morgott, The Omen King

The fifth shardbearer you encounter, Morgott is a demigod, and the sole leader of Leyndell, the Royal Capital. He is known as "The Omen King" Due to being the most powerful Omen, a creature from the game which is usually covered in spikes.

  • Health- 10,399 HP
  • Defense- 114
  • Special Attack- Cursed Explosion
